Our Courses

Courses We Offer

We have a variety of courses to choose from: Technology, Web Development, Health, English, and a whole lot more. Whatever your path is, we most likely have it ready for you.

Intermediate

If you already have your prerequisite courses out of the way and ready to take a shot at more advanced levels, then here are the courses for you. This will require much focus and stronger discipline but in the end you will be rewarded with a greater education than you've ever had.

Degree

Our degrees are certified but the highest educational standards that are widely recognized from UC to Yale. Any number of degrees can be achieved via online or in-person.

Post Graduation

Congratulations on your success so far! Now it's time to up the ante and begin a more specific type of education. Your post grad courses are intended for you to be highly skilled in the area of expertise you want to learn from.

Our Facilities

Our headquarters are located in Sacramento, CA. We have offices on all 260 campuses worldwide. We serve all students worldwide and welcome all into our building.

World Class Library

Pick a book, any book, and we are willing to bet you will find it here. Our library is recognized by scholars as one of the best libraries in all of America, perhaps, the world.

Largest Playground

No doubt you will be transformed to one of the largest play areas in any college. Watch or play at our state-of-the-art outdoors dedicated to athletes.

Tasty and Healthy Food

Our campuses have the best food grown from local farmers. We have everything from cheeseburgers to vegan plates. All staff are michelin star chefs. You would not believe this is a real cafeteria.

About Us

Eduford University was founded in 1909 by Reginald Eduford. His vision to help the underprivileged is a vision we share today.

As of today, we have had over 2.5 million students in-person and another 1.5 online. We are excited to continue to grow and look forward to the future of our amazing students.
